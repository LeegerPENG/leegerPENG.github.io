write an article

  • open git-bash in your file
  • use command hexo new "your blog article title"
  • open your blog article title.md to edit your article (Typora is a good application for writing markdown on windows)

post an article to your blog

  • connect your local repository with remove github using SSH keys
  • hexo d -g to update your blog
